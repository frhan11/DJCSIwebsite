What is DJCSI?

At D. J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, the students of IT department have formed their own student’s chapter ‘DJSCE-CSI’ for overall development of students. The mission of the DJSCE-CSI chapter is to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals, while simultaneously inspiring and nurturing students and helping them to integrate into the IT community.
DJCSI also holds various events, some of these are:
GIT WORKSHOP
CSI WEEKEND
CODESHASTRA
PITCH PLEASE

Goals and Awards

With the help of technology, DJCSI aims to ignite a flame of curiosity in the minds of students and create a yearning for learning among them. We intend to choreograph and fabricate a platform for optimizing the technical aptitude in a fun and entertaining way in our endeavor to bridge the gap between university and industry.
